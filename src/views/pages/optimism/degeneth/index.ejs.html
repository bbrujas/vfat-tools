  the Badlands. Two, to be exact.

  treasure and in order to reach it, the journey is much shorter
  and safer.  

  The other, is much farther out. Surrounded by some of the
  the most degenerate in the Badlands. The loot is much
  greater, and so are the stakes.

  slow at getting ready for a trek across the barren land.

  least 250 $BANDIT to guide you there safely. It holds 0.5 WETH

  The farther stash is a much more treacherous journey 
  and you might lose some guides a long the way. You
  will need at least 450 $BANDIT's to ensure your safe passage.
